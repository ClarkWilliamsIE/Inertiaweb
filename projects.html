Digital Pasifika Instruments

The Digital Pasifika Instruments project is an innovative initiative aimed at blending traditional Pasifika musical instruments with modern digital technology. This project provides students with the opportunity to explore the rich cultural heritage of Pasifika music while learning about contemporary digital music production techniques.

Through this project, students will engage in hands-on activities that involve designing, creating, and programming digital interfaces for traditional instruments, allowing them to produce unique sounds and performances. This fusion of old and new encourages creativity, technical skills, and cultural appreciation.
