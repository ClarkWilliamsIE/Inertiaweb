Motion Capture Sport Analysis

Our Motion Capture Sport Analysis project is designed to revolutionize how students learn about biomechanics and physical education. By using advanced motion capture technology, students can analyze their movements in various sports, gaining insights into their performance and techniques.

This project not only enhances students' understanding of human movement but also provides a practical application of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) principles. By engaging in this innovative project, students develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a deeper appreciation for the mechanics of sports.
